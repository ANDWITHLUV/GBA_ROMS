# POCKET AUDITION
Pocket Audition may allow users to quickly and simply build instruments using the GBA's four PSG channels and test their sounds wihtin a limited range. Instruments may be able to play sounds in octaves [2,8] using keys {C , C#, D , D#, E , F , F#, G, G#, A , A#, B}. The aplication features four main secxtions: Keys, Scales, Controls, and Settings. Key bindings can be found in the settings area's right hand side. These key bindings will change as you employ them. The Gba festures four PSG channels: Duty+Sweep, Duty, Wave, and Noise. This application follows the same order acceppt the channels are offset by one. The Gba produces frequencies from user provided rates.
