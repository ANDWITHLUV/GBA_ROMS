# POCKET AUDITION
Pocket Audition may allow users to quickly and simply build instruments using the GBA's four PSG channels and test their sounds wihtin a limited range. Instruments may be able to play sounds in octaves [2,8] using keys {C , C#, D , D#, E , F , F#, G, G#, A , A#, B}.
